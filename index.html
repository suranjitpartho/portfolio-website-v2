About Me

I am an experienced data professional with over six years of expertise in data analysis, visualization, geospatial analysis, and information system development. Delivered data strategic insights and solutions for humanitarian programs like Rohingya refugee response and anti-human trafficking initiatives. I am proficient in Python programming with expertise in data science libraries, machine learning models, natural language processing, and statistical methods. I have experience in application development using the Python Django framework. Well-versed in creating interactive and automated dashboards using Power Bi. Highly adept in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), with advanced skills in ArcGIS Pro. Proficient in database management with expertise in PostgreSQL and MS Access. Additionally, I possess skills in designing compelling infographics using Illustrator and InDesign.
Currently serving as an MIS Coordinator at Winrock International, I specialize in developing and maintaining robust MIS and GIS databases using tools like PostgreSQL, MS Access, ArcGIS Pro, and Power BI. I design data collection tools (e.g., KoBo Toolbox), conduct advanced data analysis (including machine learning and spatial interpolation), and create interactive dashboards to inform critical decision-making processes. I also train and coordinate partner organizations to implement MIS systems effectively. Previously, at BRAC, I led the MIS team and was the core developer for the MEAL Integrated Platform, supporting one of the world's largest refugee response programs. My work involved database management, maintenance of the web application, GIS mapping, and crafting dynamic dashboards using Power BI to monitor project progress and outcomes. I also conducted capacity-building sessions for teams on data analysis and visualization tools.
My academic background in business provided me with foundational knowledge in various disciplines, including coursework in Statistics and Management Information Systems. These courses sparked my interest in data and information systems, inspiring me to pursue a career in this domain. Over the years, I have deepened my expertise through advanced online courses and trainings in Data Science, SQL, GIS, and Python programming, further solidifying my skills and passion for the field. In addition to my professional work, I have shared my knowledge by creating a YouTube channel where I have uploaded tutorial videos on tools like Power BI and Excel, with plans to expand this collection in the future. I am also passionate about digital arts and have explored this creative field through a few projects; here is my ArtStation profile. I am dedicated to leveraging data to generate actionable insights and enhance decision-making processes, with the ultimate goal of becoming an expert data professional and contributing my expertise to humanitarian and development initiatives.
